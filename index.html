Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about how our AI lead engine works? We've got answers.

How quickly will I start seeing results?

Most of our clients start seeing qualified leads within the first week of setup. The AI begins learning your business parameters immediately. Within 30 days, you'll have enough data to see meaningful conversion metrics. Many clients report a 40% increase in lead quality within the first month. Of course, results vary based on your industry, market, and how well you've configured your qualification criteria, but we've found that even conservative estimates show ROI within 60 days for most businesses.

How much does this cost compared to traditional advertising?

On average, our clients spend 60-75% less per qualified lead compared to Google Ads or Facebook advertising. While a typical Google Ad might cost $15-50 per click with a 2-5% conversion rate, our AI-qualified leads average $3-8 per lead with a 25-40% conversion rate. Plus, unlike ads, your costs don't increase as you scale. You're paying for a platform, not for impressions. Most small to medium businesses see a positive ROI within 2-3 months. We offer flexible pricing plans starting at $499/month, with no long-term contracts.

What if the leads aren't a good fit for my business?

This is exactly why we built in extensive customization. During your onboarding, you'll work with our team to define your ideal customer profile in detail. The AI learns from your feedback—when you mark leads as "good fit" or "not a fit," the system gets smarter and adjusts its qualification criteria accordingly. We also provide detailed analytics showing which sources and characteristics produce your best conversions. If you're consistently getting poor-quality leads, we'll help you refine your settings at no additional cost. Your success is our success.

Is my data secure and compliant with privacy laws?

Absolutely. We take data security very seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using 256-bit encryption. We're fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and other major privacy regulations. Our infrastructure is hosted on enterprise-grade servers with redundancy and automatic backups. We undergo regular third-party security audits and maintain a 99.99% uptime guarantee. We also provide you with complete control over your data—you can export it anytime, and we never sell or share your data with third parties. Your customer information belongs to you.

Can I integrate this with my existing CRM or tools?

Yes! We integrate with all major CRM platforms including HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, and many others. We also support Zapier, which connects to thousands of other tools. If you're using a custom or less common system, we provide a robust API and webhook support for custom integrations. All leads automatically sync in real-time, so your sales team always has the latest information. No manual data entry, no delays, no confusion about which system is the source of truth. Setup typically takes less than 30 minutes for standard integrations.
